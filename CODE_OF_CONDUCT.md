# Code of Conduct

This project follows a respectful and inclusive collaboration model.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Focus on technical content, not personal attacks.
- Accept feedback and discuss trade-offs with evidence.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private information without consent.
- Intentional disruption or abuse in issues/PRs.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove content that violates this policy and may block repeat offenders.
